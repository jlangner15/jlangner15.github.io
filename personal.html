Personal

Here is my personal hobby music page. Thanks to my roomates, I started playing bass in the fall of 2020 as they wanted a bass player to jam with them. I picked up a used starter bass package on kijiji, a yorkville y50b amp and a '93 yamaha rbx p/j bass. I fell in love instantly and picked it up quickly with daily practice. I played piano throughout my childhood and into high school and also played trumpet in the high school band, however I enjoy the bass far more.
